About Me

I am currently a Software Engineering Fellow at Formation, where I am expanding my skills in software development. Prior to this role, I did an internship at a start-up where I gained valuable experience in the tech industry.

Before transitioning into tech, I enjoyed working in healthcare and the rewards of making a positive impact in patients' lives. However, my passion for software engineering, nurtured through my hobby of gaming, led me to make a career change. I decided to enroll in the Grace Hopper Program at Fullstack Academy to pursue this passion further. I am excited to leverage my technical skills, along with my communication and collaboration skills, to develop projects that improve people's lives.

When I am not coding, I like to play with my husky, Broly.

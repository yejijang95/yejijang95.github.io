About Me

I'm a graduate of the Grace Hopper program at Fullstack Academy. Before enrolling in the Grace Hopper program, my background was in healthcare. Although I enjoyed working in healthcare and the rewards of making positive impact in patients' lives, I was always attracted to software engineering. I consistently felt a calling to it in my hobby of gaming. After some reflections during the height of the pandemic, I knew I wanted to pursue my passions in this field! Moving forward, I want to leverage my newfound technical skills along with my communication and collaboration skills to build projects that will improve people's lives.

When I am not coding, I like to eat dessert and play with my husky, Broly.
